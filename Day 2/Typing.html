Welcome to BlueJscript

This help will tell you what BlueJscript is not and where to go for further help

BlueJscript is not

A learning environment: Do not expect BlueJscript to teach you the syntax of Javascript. To find out about Javascript you will need to look at the references in the main BlueJscript site.

A complete browser system: Most of the BlueJscript examples have been written in standerd HTML. But they contains linkes to other side that used the most advanced HTML and they are not compatible with the standerd viewer that is embedded here. If you want to browse the internet use you preferred browser

A tutorial on Webservice: You will realize that you can drive BlueJscript through webservices. There will be examples on how to do it but you will need to do your homeworkon understanding the technology.
